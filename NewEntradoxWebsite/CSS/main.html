ABOUT
ENTRADOX
A multi-faceted team with expertise in mechanics, circuits and wiring, programming, arts, promotion, outreach and more. Our 10 member strong team works to strategize, build, program and compete with select robots. Outreach is also a major focus, in which we teach and collaborate with youth, other FTC Teams around the globe and community members.



In 2018, Meadowridge school initiated the FTC robotics program. As a rookie team, the team struggled to construct a reliable robot. Nevertheless, they learned from more experienced teams and grew into what became known as Blown Fuses Forever.



In the following season, the team rebranded to Team BFF (Blown Fuses Forever). Having grown from the previous season, BFF produced a rugged robot, reliably scoring points in major tournaments. In the 2019-20 season, BFF ranked and performed at the league average.
